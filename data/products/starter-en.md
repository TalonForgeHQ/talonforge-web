---

**How to use**: pick one, replace placeholders, post. If a template doesn't land in {72h}, try a different category — you're probably a better fit for different content types than you think. Track what sticks.

**Pairing tip**: thread starters (category 10) pair best with metrics posts (category 3) as the body. Contrarian takes (category 5) pair with founder story (category 7) to soften.

**Posting cadence**: 1 post/day is enough if 1/7 is a thread. 3 posts/day is ceiling before engagement drops per post.
