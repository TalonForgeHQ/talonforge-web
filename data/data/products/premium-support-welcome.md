## How to get the most out of support

### Ask good questions
Bad question: "It's not working, help."
Good question: "I ran the Kit, answered the 10 questions, but the generated MEMORY.md is in English even though I chose Arabic. Here's the file: [PASTE]. What did I miss?"

Specific > vague. Show what you did > show what you expected. Paste files if they're small enough.

### Batch your questions
- Daily micro-questions are welcome but think about efficiency: collect 3-5 related questions and send them together.
- You'll get faster, more structured answers.

### Tell us what worked
- When something works, tell us. Not because we need validation — because we need signal on what's replicable.
- These stories become case studies (with your permission + anonymity option) that drive our roadmap.
